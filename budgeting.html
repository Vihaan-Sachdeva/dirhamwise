📝 Budgeting

Your parents may have told you about how they need to pay bills and buy groceries and pay for other things, right? That's called Budgeting. Its when you split up your money over a period of time to cover your expenses. Adults, who most likely get pay cheques, use that as their baseline for budgeting. In this section of DirhamWise, there is a golden rule that is mostly followed, that being the 50 30 20 rule. This basically means 50% income towards needs (mortgage and/or rent, insurance, groceries, bills etc.), 30% towards wants (entertainment and sports for example) and 20% into saving. Other methods such as the 70 10 10 10 rule (70% needs, 10% wants, 10% sharing, and 10% saving) also exist (this one gives more money to immediate needs).
